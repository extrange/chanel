    France (Nice) Hiking 7-10 May 2022

    Itinerary

    7 May, Saturday

    • 4.20pm: Nicholas reaches NCE T1

    • Walk to Carrefour/Supermarche Casino to buy:

      • Dinner
      • Hiking snacks
      • Water
      • Camping fuel
      • Lighter

    • Buy bus card if necessary

    • 6.30pm: Bus 90 from Grand Arenas to La Colmiane, 1hr 35min (Google Maps Directions)
    • Overnight in hotel

    8 May, Sunday

    • Free breakfast at hotel
    • Checkout, fill water

    • Hiking route:

      • Col de Veillos, Col du Barn, Mont Pépoiri and Baus de la Frema (16.9km loop, .gpx file)
      • Optionally detour to Lacs des Millefonts (.gpx file)
      • Map of routes (Chanel)
      • Other options (AllTrails)

    • Pitch campsite by sunset (~8pm)

    • Overnight in tent, with Milky Way/stargazing

    9 May, Monday

    • If not camping, La Colmiane, Col de Varaire, Caire Gros (.gpx file) (12.4km loop) is another option in the south.
    • Begin trek back to hotel
    • Check-in, enjoy nice shower
    • 7.30pm: EUR27 Dinner with local cheese
    • Overnight in hotel

    10 May, Tuesday

    • 10.36am: Bus 90 from Saint-Dalmas to Grand Arenas, 1hr 44min (Google Maps Directions)
    • 6.40pm: Flight back to Singapore (LH1065 at NCE T1)

    Accomodation

    Misc

    Chanel Items to get/pack

    • Trekking shoes (after looking at the temperature, the brown one is fine)
    • Backpack: 30L and above, with waist strap (I can lend you)
    • Sleeping bag (I will lend you one)
    • Baselayer, preferably wool
    • Waterproof outer layer
    • Fleece/mid layer jacket
