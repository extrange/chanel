    Vietnam 13-19 Nov 2022

    Itinerary

    13 Nov

    • 910am Flight to Hanoi
    • 1140am Arrive in Hanoi
    • Flight to Dong Hoi
    • [90 min] Local bus to Phong Nha (hourly)
    • Overnight at Son River Homestay

    14 Nov

    • Cycling/Visit Phong Nha Cave (other options)
    • Go to Central Backpackers Hostel to pay for the bus?
    • Overnight at Son River Homestay

    15 Nov

    • 0700 HT1 Hang Tien Discovery
    • 2130 Overnight bus to Ninh Bin (Central Backpackers Hostel, 30min from Son River Homestay)

    16 Nov

    • 0400 Arrive Ninh Bin
    • Explore Ninh Bin, climb Hang Mua Peak, rent bicycles
    • Search for river cruise option, otherwise book on Klook
    • Train (2.5hr) /Limo van (1.5hr) to Hanoi (bus not recommended)
    • Overnight in Hanoi

    17 Nov

    • ~0800 River cruise (possibly hotel pickup)
    • Kayak/swimming/explore Cat Ba island
    • Overnight in cruise

    18 Nov

    • Morning in cruise
    • ~1200 Back in Hanoi
    • ~1300 Head to Old Quarter
    • Explore 36 ancient streets, Sword Lake, Night market
    • R&R/Fattening/Night market/Spa
    • Try Pizza 4P
    • Overnight at La Passion Hotel & Spa

    19 Nov

    • 0900 [45min] Bus 86 or airport shuttle to Airport
    • 1130am Flight to Singapore

    Options

    • Halong Bay night cruise
    • Ninh Binh
    • Tram coc
    • Trang an
    • Caves
    • Rice fields
    • Phong Nha camping
    • Camping places
    • Spa/Massage

    Caving Notes

    HT2 Hang Tien Endeavor is available from 16-17 Nov for SGD $348 pp, and appears to provide better night sky views than TL2 Cave Encounter.

    Food to eat

    • Hanoi nomnoms 1 and nomnoms 2:
      • Bánh cuốn Yên Phụ (Steamed rice rolls)
      • Bún riêu Hồng Phúc (Crab cake rice noodle)
      • BÚN ỐC GIANG (Snail Noodles)
      • BÁNH MÌ 25 (Bánh mì)
      • BÚN ĐẬU MẸT (Vermicelli noodles with tofu and rice pork cake with shrimp paste)
      • XÔI RÁN HÀNG ĐIẾU (Fried Sticky Rice)
      • Egg Coffee Giảng
